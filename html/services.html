Services

Our Services

01

Consultations

We offer consultations for diagnostic and treatment purposes from various healthcare professionals - including doctors, physiotherapists and occupational therapists. Our doctors will be there for you on every step of your healthcare journey and are passionate about making a difference in your healthcare journey.

02

COVID-19 Testing

We offer both PCR and rapid covid testing services. PCR results within 24 hours and rapid test results within 15 minutes. Also available for travel purposes.

03

Blood tests

We do blood tests for diagnostic purposes. Blood tests can check for a variety of health markers - vitamins, minerals, infections. They can help your doctor find out bout the health of your kidneys, heart, liver and thyroid. They also help with the diagnosis of cancer, diabetes, coronary heart diseas and HIV/AIDS.

04

X-Rays

We offer x-ray services for diagnostic purposes. X-rays are a form of electromagnetic radiation that can penetrate the human body and produce images of the internal structures of the body. X-rays are used to diagnose and treat a variety of conditions.

05

Ultrasound

We offer ultrasound services for diagnostic purposes. Ultrasound is a form of medical imaging that uses high frequency sound waves to produce images of the internal structures of the body. Ultrasound is used to diagnose and treat a variety of conditions.

06

Radiology

We perform all types of radiology - X-Rays, CT scans, MRI's and ultrasound. It is used to diagnose or treat patients by recording images of the internal structure of the body to assess the presence or absence of disease, foreign objects, and structural damage or anomaly.
