About Us

Better living has provided interdisciplinary treatment since March 2012. Dr Lienka Botha & Partners is the group practice provider (General Practitioners and Physiotherapists) that provide the DBC Active Spinal Rehabilitation program.

Over the past decade our practice has combined interdisciplinary skills and knowledge to treat our patients with evidence-based medicine.
